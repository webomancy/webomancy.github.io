About Webomancy

We started Webomancy as two friends at 16, because we hated seeing good businesses stuck with bad websites. What began as a passion project in our college dorm has grown into a dedicated team of designers and developers.

We custom-build everything from scratch — no shortcuts, no templates — so your brand stands out like it should. Our approach combines technical excellence with artistic sensibility to create digital experiences that truly resonate.

We're based in Kerala, but work with clients all over India who appreciate craftsmanship and attention to detail.
